Submit a Wedding

We love to feature Real Weddings , and Real Brides who are style icons for brides to be. So if you were a part of a beautiful wedding  whether a budget bonanza or a luxury affair, write in to us !

Submit a Wedding

  • Write in to mehak (at) wedmegood (dot) com with 5-10 photos of the wedding and we shall get back to you
  • Any one is free to submit a wedding- brides, grooms, photographers (even those not on our showcase are welcome). wedding planners and more
  • What we look for: Great photos showing detail. Photos showing the brides full length outfit, the decor, close up shot of table arrangements, shots of details in the wedding like a photobooth or a unique personalised element, shots of gifts, shots of the grooms outfit, jewellery etc. We need to see DETAIL !
